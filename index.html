ABOUT

I was born in San Francisco in 1992. Since then I've been drawing and making stuff using whatever tools I can find. Lately programming has been particularly important to me.

I have worked at lots of companies. Hedge funds, social media companies, SaaS companies, television studios, public companies, private companies, billion dollar companies, startups that can't afford heat, all sorts of things.

My other interests include boomerang throwing, kite surfing and being a terrible musician. I am also teaching myself to read ancient Egyptian hieroglyphs. Lately I have also been getting into rocketry and weather balloon launches. Three things I couldn't live without are coffee, art, and my mac :)

I am currently on an extended sojourn in Cleveland, Ohio.
